Toronto Raptors
  • A Canadian professional basketball team based in Toronto
  • The Raptors compete in the National Basketball Association (NBA) as a member of the league's Eastern Conference Atlantic Division
  • They play their home games at Scotiabank Arena, which they share with the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League (NHL)
  • The team was founded in 1995 as part of the NBA's expansion into Canada, along with the Vancouver Grizzlies
  • Their mascot is a Velociraptor, based off of the movie Jurassic Park
